I.About Us

Founded in 2007 and headquartered in Shanghai, Ivy Capital is among China’s first market-oriented venture capital firms. Across multiple market cycles, we have remained driven by in-depth research and industry insight, seeking out entrepreneurs with the capability, ambition, and resilience to unlock vast market value.

Ivy operates both a primary-market platform, “Ivy Capital,” and a secondary-market platform, “Ivy Fund,” with combined assets under management exceeding RMB 24 billion. We have invested in and accompanied more than 150 technology companies over the long term, 23 of which have gone public.

ii.Investment Strategy

// A Deep-Tech Fund Built on Long-Termism

Ivy Capital focuses on deep tech, with particular emphasis on AI (software and hardware), chips and semiconductors, and intelligent manufacturing — continually seeking teams that command durable technological barriers and lasting industrial value. We invest primarily at the early stage, from angel through growth rounds, with a commitment to long-term partnership and continued follow-on backing.
