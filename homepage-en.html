I.About Us

Founded in 2007 and headquartered in Shanghai, Ivy Capital is among China’s first Private Sector Venture Capital firms. Over almost twenty years, we have remained driven by in-depth research and industry insight, seeking out entrepreneurs with the resilience, ambition, and potential to build world-class companies.

Ivy operates in both primary and secondary markets through our platforms “Ivy Capital” and “Ivy Assets,” with combined AUM exceeding RMB 30 billion. We have invested in and accompanied more than 200 tech companies, 30 of which have gone public.
