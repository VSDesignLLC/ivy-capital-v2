## 2. About (Page 2)
- Page intro:
  > Early-stage investment focused on deep tech, with a global perspective — dedicated to identifying and backing the world's most outstanding technology companies.
- Investment focus: AI (Software & Hardware) / Chips & Semiconductors / Intelligent Manufacturing
- Investment Philosophy:
  - **Integrity** — We act with integrity and principle, engaging every entrepreneur, investor, and partner in good faith, and building trusted relationships for the long term.
  - **Vision** — We invest in the future with foresight, believing that the technologies destined to change the world are, in their earliest days, rarely understood by the many.
  - **Yearning** — We set out from passion and curiosity, believing in humanity's exploration of the unknown, its pursuit of science, and the courage to create the future.
- Industry Recognition: see §1 · IV.
- Post-Investment Empowerment:
  - **A Top-Tier Brain Trust** — Over many years we have built a CXO Network of accomplished entrepreneurs, scientists, seasoned investors, and leading think-tank scholars. We offer more than boardroom advice: we bring to your side a cohort of leaders who have truly been in the arena, at the technological frontier, so that at a company's pivotal stages this "external brain" can provide founders with cross-disciplinary, in-depth counsel — ensuring that building a company is never a solitary endeavor.
  - **Deep Ecosystem Synergy** — Beyond capital, we help portfolio companies connect with upstream and downstream supply chains, key customers, listed companies, and strategic partners. Drawing on Ivy's long-standing presence across both primary and secondary markets, together with our extensive industrial-ecosystem resources, we also foster collaboration among our portfolio companies — bringing technology to genuine industrial fruition.
  - **A Partner Who Runs Alongside the Founder** — Often, we serve as a founder's confidant, grounded in a deep empathy for the solitude and the low points of entrepreneurship. When the nights are hardest and giving up feels near, we are the ones ready to answer the call at any hour and stand with you. We understand how demanding the journey is; we are here not only to win with you, but — at your most difficult moments — to offer unreserved support and unwavering conviction.
